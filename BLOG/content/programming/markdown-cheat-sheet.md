Date: 2014-07-02 20:21

- *emphasis* or _italics_
- **strong** or __bold__
- ~~strikethrough~~

``` 
<em> is *emphasis* or _italics_
<strong> is **strong** or __bold__
<del> is ~~strikethrough~~
```
